Goal

To aggregate and organise recordings of technical Scala talks from meetups and conferences around the globe.
Hopefully it will help in learning and spreading the knowledge.

Open Source and Roadmap

Built using: akka-http, angular 4, docker, kubernetes, scala.
APIs used: getpocket.com, vimeo.com, youtube.com.

All sources and roadmap are available at github.com/scala-fun.

Content ownership

The videos on scala.fun are aggregated from a publicly available recordings on Youtube and Vimeo.
They are not hosted here and are embedded inline with terms and conditions of those services.
We are not claiming ownership of any displayed videos - all right and and responsibilities stays with video owners.
You can get to original by clicking service icon on bottom right corner of the video.

If you own the content and are and not happy for your video to be listed here - please contact at spikerlabs.com.
